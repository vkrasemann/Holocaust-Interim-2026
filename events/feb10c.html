One option is The Polish "Pizza Baguette".

Zapiekanka is a long, open-faced toasted baguette topped with savory ingredients. It originated in the communist era and has become a cult favorite among locals and tourists alike. Typical Toppings: Sautéed mushrooms and melted cheese (classic base)
Optional extras: ham, salami, onions, pickles, jalapeños, or even kebab meat
Generously drizzled with ketchup, garlic sauce, or spicy sauces
