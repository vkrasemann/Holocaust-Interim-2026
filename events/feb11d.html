In Kazimierz we have the choice of various street food places, for instance, for a broad street food experience, we can visit Judah Food Market, a vibrant food truck park in Kazimierz offering a diverse range of cuisines, from traditional Polish dishes to international flavors. Or Okrąglak is a circular building surrounded by numerous food stalls, each offering their own take on the beloved Polish street food, zapiekanka. This open-faced baguette, traditionally topped with mushrooms and cheese, has evolved to include a variety of toppings such as meats, vegetables, and flavorful sauces
